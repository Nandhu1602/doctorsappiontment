AccuHealth Department of Dentistry

Dentistry is the branch of medicine that deals with the study, prevention, management, and treatment of conditions and diseases affecting the oral cavity, especially the teeth, jaws, and gums.

Our Assurance

  • Quick identification of dental issues
  • A perfect blend of predictability and high-quality clinical delivery
  • Maintaining and promoting the highest dental healthcare standards
  • Providing financially viable options to all our patients
  • Our expert panel comprises general dentists, pediatric dentists, orthodontists, periodontists, oral surgeons, endodontists, and prosthodontists. You get the right match for your type of dental problem.
  • We offer comprehensive corrective, restorative, cosmetic, and complete mouth reconstruction rehabilitation services.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a serious procedure that removes bacteria from an infected tooth, preventing further decay and reinfections of the tooth by cleaning, filling, and sealing them. At AccuHealth, you have the best team looking into your dental issues. People choose us because –

  • We have the most qualified doctors in our panel
  • RCT is painless at AccuHealth
  • We guarantee you a beautiful and lasting result

Bridge/Crown

Dental crowns are used to repair damaged teeth and are placed on top of a dental implant to replace a missing tooth. Also followed by RCT ; to strengthen the tooth functionality. A dental bridge, on the other hand, is used to replace missing teeth.

  • Our specialists help you in choosing the best material crowns and bridges depending on your needs and affordability.
  • We assist in giving you as real a smile as possible and stable support without you worrying about the artificial fixtures sliding and slipping off.

Extraction

Broken or impacted teeth, tooth infection and decay, loose teeth, presence of wisdom teeth, and overcrowded teeth can necessitate tooth extraction in children and adults.

  • We conduct both simple and surgical extraction with clear post-operative instructions.
  • The treatment guidelines are systematic and dispel all patient doubts and misgivings.
  • Detailed discussions are held with the patient to understand their medical history prior to the procedure
  • Doctors are available 24*7 to attend any kind of post-procedural discomfort.

Scaling and Polishing

Teeth scaling and polishing are dental procedures used to remove accumulated debris and other deposits that create ideal conditions for the bacteria to thrive on and damage teeth as well as surrounding structures such as gum, bone support to the particular tooth/teeth.

  • Our experts render your teeth clean and bright, smooth, and healthy.
  • Specialized instruments are used to gently remove plaque and tartar effectively, leaving you with a beautiful-looking healthy smile

Removable Flexible Denture

They are removable dental appliances that replace missing or damaged teeth. They are constructed from a variety of advanced materials. We guarantee dentures that are-

  • Flexible
  • Aesthetically satisfying
  • Comfortable and convenient to wear
  • Do not absorb stains or cause any further complications for the teeth and surrounding structures.

Denture Repair

We fix broken dentures. Though dentures are meant to be reasonably stable and durable, sometimes they could be impacted by hard bites, excessive pressure, and depreciation. Damaged dentures can irritate the gums, causing soreness and infections, besides other oral problems such as oral ulcer which is if neglected can lead to oral cancer.

  • AccuHealth has skilled technicians to handle perfect repairs with a quick turnaround time

Dental X-Ray

Dental X-rays are done with low radiation levels to take an inside view of your teeth-surrounding bone structure to help the doctor examine gum and teeth problems and determine your overall oral health.

  • All types of dental x-rays are done – intraoral x-rays like bitewing, occlusal by appointment .
